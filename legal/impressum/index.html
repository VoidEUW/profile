7. Rights of Data Subjects

Every data subject has the right at any time to:

  • Access the personal data stored about them (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Rectification of inaccurate data (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Erasure of their data (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Object to processing (Art. 21 GDPR)

There is also the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority.
